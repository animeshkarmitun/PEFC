Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the free consultation? +

A 45-minute 1-on-1 session with a European International counsellor where we assess your academic profile, discuss your goals, budget, and preferred destinations, and provide a shortlist of 3 suitable universities. No obligation, no hidden fees.

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes. For the Complete and Premium packages, you can pay 50% upfront and 50% after receiving your first university offer letter. We also accept bank transfers, mobile banking, and card payments.

What if my visa gets rejected? +

Our 96.7% success rate means rejections are rare. But if it happens, we analyze the refusal reason, refile with corrected documentation at no extra charge, or help you pivot to an alternative destination. We don't abandon you.

Do you guarantee university admission? +

No ethical consultancy can guarantee admission — universities make final decisions. However, we only recommend institutions where your profile is competitive, and we have a 94% offer-letter rate because of our rigorous pre-assessment.

Can I upgrade my package later? +

Absolutely. You can start with the free consultation, upgrade to Complete when you're ready to apply, and move to Premium anytime before your visa filing. You only pay the difference between packages.
