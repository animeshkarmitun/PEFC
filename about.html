About PFEC Global

Since 2006, we've been bridging dreams and destinations — guiding students from Bangladesh toward world-class education across 11 countries.

06

Founded in 2006

From a single desk in Dhaka to a global network of 550+ university partnerships across four countries.

Our Story

Turning Ambitions into Admissions

PFEC Global began with a simple belief: every deserving student deserves access to world-class education, regardless of geography or background. What started as a small counselling office in Dhanmondi has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted study-abroad consultancies.

Today, we operate four offices across Bangladesh, with partner networks in Australia, India, Sri Lanka, and beyond. Our team of 80+ counsellors, visa specialists, and career advisors has helped over 22,000 students secure placements at top-tier institutions worldwide.
