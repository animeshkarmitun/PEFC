Our Story

Turning Ambitions into Admissions

European International began with a simple belief: every deserving student deserves access to world-class education, regardless of geography or background. What started as a small counselling office in Dhanmondi has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted study-abroad consultancies.

Today, we operate four offices across Bangladesh, with partner networks in Australia, India, Sri Lanka, and beyond. Our dedicated team of counsellors, visa specialists, and career advisors has helped over 22,000 students secure placements at top-tier institutions worldwide.
