Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What kind of universities do you work with?

+

We partner with over 550+ renowned institutions globally, including top-tier universities in the UK, Australia, Canada, USA, and across Europe. Our partners range from prestigious research universities to specialized vocational colleges.

How many students does European International serve?

+

Since 2006, we have successfully assisted more than 22,000+ students in achieving their dreams of studying abroad. Our dedicated team of experts provides personalized support to hundreds of new students every month.

Is the UK education system good?

+

The UK education system is world-renowned for its high standards and prestigious qualifications. UK universities consistently rank among the best globally, offering a diverse range of courses and excellent career prospects for international students.

Is there any fee required for our services?

+

We offer FREE initial consultations and guidance. Our goal is to make your study abroad journey as accessible as possible. Specific service fees may apply for advanced visa processing or premium application support, which will be transparently discussed.
