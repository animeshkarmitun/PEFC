Insider Tips

How to Maximize Your Scholarship Chances

🎯

Start Early

Most scholarships have deadlines 8–12 months before intake. Starting early gives time to strengthen your profile and craft a winning application.

📄

Tailor Every Essay

Generic essays get rejected. We help you customize each application to the scholarship's specific mission, values, and selection criteria.

🏆

Highlight Impact

Scholarship committees care about what you'll do AFTER graduation. Show them how you'll contribute to Bangladesh's development.

Don't Leave Money on the Table

Every year, millions in scholarship funding goes unclaimed because students don't know they qualify. Let European International find your match.
