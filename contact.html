Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free? +

Yes — absolutely. Your first consultation with a European International counsellor is 100% free with no hidden charges or obligations. We believe every student deserves access to expert guidance before making any financial commitment.

How long does the entire process take? +

From first consultation to university admission, the process typically takes 3–6 months. Visa processing adds another 4–12 weeks depending on the destination country. We provide a personalised timeline during your first meeting.

Do I need to visit an office, or can we do everything online? +

Both options work. Many students complete the entire process via video calls and document uploads. However, we recommend at least one in-person visit for visa document verification and biometric guidance.

What documents do I need for the first meeting? +

Just your academic transcripts (SSC/HSC or equivalent) and a valid passport if you have one. Don't worry if documents are incomplete — we'll create a checklist tailored to your target universities.

Do you guarantee visa approval? +

No consultancy can legally guarantee a visa. However, our 96.7% visa grant rate speaks for itself. We pre-assess every application against embassy criteria and only submit when we're confident. If there are red flags, we tell you honestly.

Can you help with scholarships? +

Yes — scholarship guidance is one of our core services. We maintain a database of 200+ active scholarships across our partner universities and help you craft compelling applications. Many of our students secure partial or full funding.
